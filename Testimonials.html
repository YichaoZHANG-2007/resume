Recommendation from Professor Xin Hong

Mentor of Youth Science Talent Program

Institution: Zhejiang University

Field: Chemistry & Interdisciplinary Research

Professor Xin Hong is a Tenured Associate Professor of Chemistry at Zhejiang University, Fellow of the Royal Society of Chemistry (FRSC), and currently serving as the Goering Visiting Professor at the University of Wisconsin-Madison. He also works as an Associate Editor for Digital Discovery (Royal Society of Chemistry). He supervised my research during the Youth Science Talent Program, guiding me through quantitative structure-activity relationship modeling of chemical reactions. His letter evaluates my research potential, analytical skills, and dedication to academic exploration.

Email: hxchem@zju.edu.cn
